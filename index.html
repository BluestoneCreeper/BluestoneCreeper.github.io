Basic Rules

-Press R to roll, click on a piece to select it and click on a highlighted possible move to move
-If you roll a 1 or a 6 you may get a piece out by clicking on it in spawn
-If a piece lands on another, the piece it landed on goes back home
-If a 6 is rolled, the player can re-roll
-If a piece roles a 1 from the star circle, it can move to the middle.
-Roll a 1 to get out of the middle, you can be sent back to spawn in the middle

How To Win

Get all your pieces back to home to win, or, be the last one willing to play

Spin Rules

"Spin! more parts the better!"

-If a piece lands on a inner space aka the star circle, spin strength increases by 1
-When a piece moves to the middle, the board spins counter clockwise corresponding to spinstrength
-When spun, spinstrength resets to 0

Save and Load

-Up arrow to save a file (overights current save file)
-Down arrow on "select number of players" screen or in a game with the same number of players as the save to load
